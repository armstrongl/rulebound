---
title: "Plain Language"
description: "Use simple, clear words that every reader understands"
---

Write for clarity, not complexity. Replace jargon, business-speak, and unnecessarily long phrases with simpler alternatives.

## Principles

- Use short words over long ones: "use" not "utilize."
- Use common words over technical ones when writing for a general audience.
- Eliminate filler: "to" instead of "in order to."
- Write at a reading level your newest team member can follow.
